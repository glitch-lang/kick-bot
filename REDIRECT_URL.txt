Kick Developer App Redirect URI:

For Local Development:
http://localhost:3000/auth/kick/callback

For Production (replace with your domain):
https://yourdomain.com/auth/kick/callback

IMPORTANT: 
- Copy this EXACTLY as shown (including http/https, port number, and path)
- Add this to your Kick Developer App settings at https://dev.kick.com
- Make sure it matches exactly in both the Kick app settings AND your .env file
